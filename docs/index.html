sikshamitra

Sanskrit: "friend of phonetic learning"

A desktop editor for Vedic and classical Sanskrit text, with automatic application of traditional phonological rules rooted in the Siksha tradition.

Download

What it is

sikshamitra is a specialized desktop editor built for scholars, students, and practitioners working with Vedic and classical Sanskrit. It automatically applies the phonological rules described in the Siksha texts and Paninian grammar tradition -- anusvara transformations, visarga sandhi, svarabhakti, holdings, pauses, and svara accents -- so you can focus on the text, not the mechanics.

Developed by Veda Union.

Features

Vedic Grammar Engine

Automatic anusvara and visarga transformations, samyukta holdings, and pause detection -- all rooted in traditional Siksha principles.

Svara Accent System

Full support for the three Vedic pitch accents: anudatta, svarita, and udatta, rendered with proper combining diacritics.

Multiple Text Sources

Tradition-aware processing for Rigveda, Yajurveda, Krishnayajurveda, and Smriti texts, each with distinct phonological behavior.

Import & Export

Import from sanskritdocuments.org and DOCX files. Export to richly formatted HTML or DOCX for publication and sharing.

Rich Text Editing

Built on Quill.js with custom blots for holdings, change-style annotations, and svara accents. Paragraph styles for titles, translations, and comments.

.smdoc Format

Custom document format with LZMA compression for efficient storage. Full support for audio attachments, metadata, and styling.

Light & Dark Themes

Ochre and saffron palette with full dark mode support. Designed for extended reading sessions with minimal eye strain.

Offline & Private

Runs entirely on your desktop. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Your texts stay on your machine.
