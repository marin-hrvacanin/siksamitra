śikṣāmitra

śikṣā "phonetics" + mitra "friend" — a companion for learning Vedic recitation

A desktop editor for transcribing, annotating, and studying Vedic texts in IAST transliteration. It applies the traditional phonological rules of Śikṣā automatically, so you can focus on the text itself.

Download View Source

śikṣāmitra implements the principles of Śikṣā — the Vedāṅga of phonetics — covering varṇa (letters), svara (pitch accent), mātrā (duration), balam (force), sāma (linking), and santāna (continuity). It transliterates Devanagari to IAST and applies anusvāra transformations, visarga sandhi, samyukta holdings, svarabhakti, pause detection, and svara accent marks — all according to the tradition you select.

The editor supports Ṛgveda, Yajurveda, Kṛṣṇayajurveda, and Smṛti source modes, each with distinct phonological behavior. Import texts from sanskritdocuments.org or shlokam.org, or type your own.

Vedic Grammar Engine

Anusvāra and visarga sandhi, samyukta holdings, svarabhakti epenthesis, pause detection — a complete pipeline rooted in Śikṣā.

Svara Accent System

Anudātta, svarita, and udātta marks with proper Unicode combining diacritics. Automatic svara placement by chandas pattern.

Source-Aware Processing

Ṛgveda, Yajurveda, Kṛṣṇayajurveda, and Smṛti modes — each with tradition-specific anusvāra and accent behavior.

Import and Export

Import Devanagari texts with svaras from sanskritdocuments.org. Export to formatted HTML or DOCX for sharing and publication.

Rich Text Editing

Paragraph styles for titles, sections, translations, and comments. Holdings, pause marks, and change-style annotations.

Offline and Private

Runs entirely on your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Documents stored locally in compressed .smdoc format.
